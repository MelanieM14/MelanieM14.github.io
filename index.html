INDOOR PLANTS

Indoor plants, apart from hydroponic gardens and air plants, will need an airy, good quality and fast-draining potting mix. A good all-purpose potting mix for houseplants may include compost or worm castings, coco coir or peat moss, perlite, vermiculite and some coarse sand or store-bought potting soil.


OUTDOOR PLANTS

Outdoor plants are a bit different from indoor plants. They need to be able to withstand the elements, so they need to be tougher. Outdoor plants will not thrive if they are kept indoors. Plus, outdoor plants require more maintenance than indoor plants.


TROPICAL PLANTS

Tropical Plant refers to any type of plant that naturally flourishes in an environment that resembles the Tropics, a region of the Earth surrounding the equator. Tropical plants require consistently warmer temperatures and more humid environments than temperate climate plants
